ABOUT US

Here at 3amStudios our goal is to provide you with high quality, eligant streetwear clothing at an affordable
price. Everyone of our peices are designed and manufactured in Toronto, Canada. We take pride in manufacturing
our items locally because it ensures better working conditions for our employees and reduces CO2 emmissions.

3amStudios was established in December 2022 by myself, Ben Mascarenhas and my younger brother, Sam Mascarenhas.
We grew up in Cambridge, Ontario a suburban city, west of Toronto. In our area there aren't many places for
young adults to shop for stylish, long lasting, streetwear type clothing. My brother and I would order our
clothes online paying insane amounts of money for shipping, duties, and taxes. Then in Summer 2022 my brother
and I wanted to start our own local brand. We utilized Virgil Abloh's website to get started, and build our brand. We first started selling t-shirts out of our trunk and eventually moved to an online store to help
expand our brand. We have spent the last few months designing our 2023 SPRING COLLECTION that will be
released on March 20th. We hope you enjoy our collection half as much as we enjoyed creating it! Peace and Love!
